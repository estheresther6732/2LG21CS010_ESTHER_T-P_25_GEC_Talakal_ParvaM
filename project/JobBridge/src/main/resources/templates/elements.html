Every job seeker should know the value of a well-crafted resume and the power of a tailored application. When applying to roles in tech, highlighting your core skills like Java, SQL, or React is a must. It's not just about what you know — it’s about how you present it.

Remember that submitting your resume to multiple portals can lead to missed opportunities if you don’t track your applications. Keep a list with deadlines and follow-ups to stay ahead.

Also, don’t forget the little details — even mentioning contributions to open-source projects or internships can boost your profile. Stay confident, stay consistent, and you’ll land the job meant for you.
